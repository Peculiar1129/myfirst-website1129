How to slove "ApiAdapter model not found"?

This issue is usually due to the lack of the path to your ApiAdapter.
You can open the folder_paths.py in your Stability Matrix or anything you're using.
Open the file and add this line
"folder_names_and_paths["ipadapter"] = ([os.path.join(models_dir, "ipadapter")], supported_pt_extensions)"
This should solve the problem perfectly
